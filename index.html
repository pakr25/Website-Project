They have carefully made this website from scratch for Intro to Computer Science at Rock Canyon High School. We were asked to create a website about something that we are passionate about.   • FOODS

    • Black Bean and Sweet Potato Tacos

      Meatless meals can be both delicious and satisfying. This rich meal choice will change your perception of "healthy food". When people think of tacos, usually they think of meat and cheese. However this taco has amazing textures that you will never expect. All the way from the smokey textures of the roasted tortillas to the creamy textures of the dices avocades, this taco will have you salivating just at the thought of it. Along with the cariety of textures this taco provides a subtle sweetness from the sweet potatoes, which works fantastically with the spices and aromatics. ENJOY!

    • Butternut Squash and Black Bean Enchiladas

      Enchiladas will forever hold a special place in my heart. Just thinking of it brings back memories of home. Opening my room door to the smell of my mom's freshly made enchiladas was the highlight of my childhood. I can smell my mom's enchiladas from a mile away, and these enchiladas bring that nastolgic back. This healthy twist on a classic dish brings a whole new taste palate to your taste buds. With colorful, nutricious veggies along with the creaminess of the black beans and cheeses, this enchilada will be a perfect dish for you and you family. The sweet sensations of the butternut squash coupled with many spices provide a tasty dish for special occasions. ENJOY!
